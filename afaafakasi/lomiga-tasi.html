Tatau

Body tatau (tattoos) are a traditional part of Samoan culture. Traditional tatau include the malu, pronounced 'mah-loo', and the pe'a, pronounced 'beh-ah'. The malu covers the upper legs from the knee and the pe'a covers the body from the knees to the middle of the back.
